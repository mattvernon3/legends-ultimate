Scoring System

Below is the scoring structure for the Legends Trophy.

  • Four points for 1st.
  • Three points for 2nd.
  • Two points for 3rd.
  • Sarasota Sunset will add one point to each of the above, and two points for 4th.
  • One point for attendance.
  • One point for each 2 FMP.
